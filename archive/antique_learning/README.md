# Antique Learning Archive

This directory contains all documents created by Manus AI and ChatGPT during
the prior development phase. They are preserved here in full — nothing is deleted.

## Why They Are Here

These documents were created through a process of AI-to-AI inflation:
one AI built claims on top of a previous AI's claims, without experimental
validation. Many contain inflated numbers, unverified assertions, and
speculation presented as fact.

They are valuable as a record of the development process and contain
real insights alongside the inflated claims. When the quality experiment
is complete, individual documents may be rehabilitated to `research/validated/`
if their core claims are supported.

## The Standard for Return

A document can move from here to the live tree when:
1. Its claims are supported by the quality experiment results, OR
2. Its claims are clearly labeled as aspirational/hypothetical, OR
3. It is historical documentation (transcripts, session records)

## What to Use Instead

For current, validated work, see:
- `research/VALIDATED_FINDINGS.md` — what we know
- `research/MATHEMATICAL_AUDIT.md` — what the math says
- `experiments/BENCHMARK_ANALYSIS.md` — what the benchmarks show
- `experiments/theos_validation_experiment.py` — the quality experiment

*From truth we build more truth.*
