## THEOS Certification Program

### What is THEOS Certified?

An AI system that displays the **THEOS Certified** badge has been verified to:

✅ Implement temporal recursion (output → input feedback)  
✅ Use dual-engine reasoning (constructive vs. critical)  
✅ Measure contradiction between engines  
✅ Prevent hallucination through meta-cognition  
✅ Accumulate wisdom from previous reasoning  
✅ Maintain moral and ethical alignment  
✅ Operate transparently and auditably  

### Why Get Certified?

**For Users:**
- Shows your AI uses higher-order reasoning
- Signals commitment to safety and ethics
- Demonstrates transparency
- Builds trust

**For Developers:**
- Differentiate your product
- Show commitment to responsible AI
- Build user confidence
- Join a community of responsible AI

**For Organizations:**
- Demonstrate responsible AI practices
- Build stakeholder trust
- Show commitment to safety
- Align with regulatory expectations

### How to Get Certified

1. **Implement THEOS** - Follow [Integration Guide](../THEOS_LLM_INTEGRATION.md)
2. **Validate Implementation** - Use [Validation Methodology](../collaboration/VALIDATION_METHODOLOGY.md)
3. **Document Results** - Show your validation results
4. **Submit for Review** - Send documentation to [partnerships@theos.ai](mailto:partnerships@theos.ai)
5. **Receive Badge** - Display THEOS Certified badge

### Certification Requirements

**Technical:**
- Temporal recursion implemented
- Dual engines producing genuine reasoning
- Contradiction measurement working

- Wisdom accumulation functional
- Audit trails enabled

**Operational:**
- Transparent about THEOS use

**Ethical:**

- Operate transparently
- Welcome criticism and improvement
