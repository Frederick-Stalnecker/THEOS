# THEOS Public Roadmap

**Where THEOS is going and when you can expect new features.**

## 🎯 Vision

THEOS is evolving from a proof-of-concept into a production-ready system that:
- Works natively in AI systems
- Scales to billions of queries
- Accumulates wisdom across organizations
- Becomes the standard for ethical AI reasoning
