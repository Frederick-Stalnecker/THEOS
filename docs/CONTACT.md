## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How long does it take to integrate THEOS?
**A:** Typically 1-2 weeks for basic integration, depending on your system complexity.

### Q: Can I use THEOS commercially?
**A:** Yes, under the MIT License. For enterprise support, contact [partnerships@theos.ai](mailto:partnerships@theos.ai).

### Q: Is THEOS production-ready?
**A:** Yes. It's been tested and validated. See [Demonstration Results](../DEMONSTRATION_RESULTS.md).

### Q: How do I report a bug?
**A:** File an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/Frederick-Stalnecker/THEOS/issues) or email [support@theos.ai](mailto:support@theos.ai).

### Q: Can I contribute code?
**A:** Yes! See [Contributing](../CONTRIBUTING.md) for guidelines.

### Q: How do I get certified?
**A:** See [Certification Program](LICENSING_AND_CERTIFICATION.md) for details.

### Q: What's the roadmap?
**A:** See [Roadmap](../ROADMAP.md) for planned features and improvements.
